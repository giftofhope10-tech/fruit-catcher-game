Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Fruit Catcher Game ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Our game is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Progress Data: High scores and game settings are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No Account Required: You can play our game without creating an account or providing any personal details.

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your high scores
  • Remember your game preferences (sound settings, difficulty level)
  • Provide a seamless gaming experience

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or app data.

Third-Party Services

Our game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Parents can feel confident that their children can enjoy our game safely.

Offline Functionality

Fruit Catcher Game works completely offline. Once installed, you can play without an internet connection. No data is transmitted over the network during gameplay.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Email: support@fruitcatchergame.com

Your Consent
