Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Fruit Catcher Game ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Our game is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Progress Data: High scores and game settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Personal Information: We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No Account Required: You can play our game without creating an account or providing any personal details.

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your high scores
  • Remember your game preferences (sound settings, difficulty level)
  • Provide a seamless gaming experience

Data Storage

All game progress data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your app data in your device settings.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Fruit Catcher Game displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a mobile advertising platform provided by Google LLC. Ads are only shown inside the Android app and are only loaded when an internet connection is available.

  • AdMob App ID: ca-app-pub-9600331042737400~8191803713
  • Banner Ads (Ad Unit ID: ca-app-pub-9600331042737400/6924594128) are displayed at the bottom of the screen while you play.
  • Interstitial Ads (Ad Unit ID: ca-app-pub-9600331042737400/3978866581) are full-screen ads that may appear periodically, such as after a game over.
  • Device Identifiers: Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID (GAID) to serve and measure relevant ads.
  • Usage Data: AdMob may collect information about your in-app activity, device type, OS version, IP address, and approximate location to serve and measure ads.
  • Personalized Ads: Google may use your data to display personalized ads. You can opt out by visiting Google Ads Settings or by resetting your device advertising ID in your Android settings under Google > Ads.
  • Offline Use: No ads are loaded or displayed when you are offline. The game works fully offline without ads.

For full details on how Google AdMob processes data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy FAQ

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is configured with tagForChildDirectedTreatment: false and tagForUnderAgeOfConsent: false in the AdMob SDK. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Cookies

We do not use cookies in the Android app. The game does not set any cookies on your device.

Offline Functionality

Fruit Catcher Game works fully offline. Advertisements require an internet connection and will not be displayed when you are offline.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access information held about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to data portability

To opt out of personalized ads, visit Google Ads Settings or use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool. On Android, you can also reset your advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings under Google > Ads.
