Introduction

Welcome to Fruit Catcher Game ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application and website.

Information We Collect

Our game is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Progress Data: High scores and game settings are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Personal Information: We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No Account Required: You can play our game without creating an account or providing any personal details.

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Provide a seamless gaming experience

Data Storage

All game progress data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or app data.
