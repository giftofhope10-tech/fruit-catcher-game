Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Fruit Catcher Game ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game on Android.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide a smooth gaming experience:

  • Game Data (Local Only): High scores, sound preferences, and difficulty settings are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Account Required: You can play without registering or logging in.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Fruit Catcher Game is free to play and is supported by advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). Ads are displayed only inside the Android app and only when an internet connection is available.

Google AdMob may automatically collect the following data to serve and measure ads:

  • Device Advertising ID (GAID): A resettable identifier used to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, and language settings.
  • IP Address: Used to determine your approximate geographic region for ad targeting.
  • App Activity: Information about how you interact with the app, used to improve ad relevance.

Types of ads shown:

  • Banner Ads — displayed at the bottom of the screen during gameplay.
  • Interstitial Ads — full-screen ads that may appear at natural transition points, such as after a game over.

Consent & Personalization:

  • If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you will be shown a consent prompt on first launch before any ads are loaded.
  • If you consent, personalized ads based on your interests may be shown.
  • If you decline, non-personalized ads will be shown instead — you will still see ads but they will not be based on your personal data.
  • Our app delays all data collection until your consent choice is made, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

Ad ID Permission: On Android 13 and above, our app requests access to the Advertising ID. You can opt out or reset your Advertising ID at any time through your device Settings → Google → Ads.

Offline Use: No ads are loaded or shown when you are offline. The game works fully without an internet connection.

For full details on how Google AdMob handles data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy & Data FAQ
  • AdMob GDPR & UMP Consent

Data Storage

All game progress data (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device using your browser or app's local storage. We have no access to this data. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device Settings → Apps → Fruit Catcher Game → Clear Data.

Children's Privacy

Fruit Catcher Game is suitable for all ages and does not contain violent or inappropriate content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Cookies

We do not use cookies in the Android app. No cookies are set on your device by our game.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to know what data is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to data portability

To manage your ad preferences:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings
  • Use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool
  • On Android: go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — Mobile advertising (Android app only). See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

Email: support@fruitcatchergame.com

Your Consent

By using Fruit Catcher Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy and the use of Google AdMob advertising as described above.
